Description

FlavorIT is a mobile app designed to make exploring and sharing culinary products easy and personalized. With advanced AI matching technology, users can search, snap, and scan items to find exact matches, while live translation features enable instant product label interpretation directly from the camera. Users can bookmark favorite products, share product links, personal recipes, and photos with friends, and customize searches by region and culture for a tailored experience. Gamification allows users to earn badges as they complete steps, enhancing engagement, while accessibility features like voice commands, multiple language options, and text-to-speech make the app inclusive and user-friendly for all.

Problem

Difficulty in finding products from our home country

Many of us face challenges related to finding products from our home country when in a foreign country.

Finding substitutes for unavailable products

In addition to the difficulty of finding products from one's home country, another challenge is identifying suitable substitutes when the exact product is not available.

Language Barriers

Not knowing the name of a particular product in the local language.

Solution

To enhance food discovery and accessibility, we developed FlavorIT!, an app focused on personalized food exploration and community engagement. FlavorIT! uses AI-powered search, snap, and scan technology to help users quickly find products by scanning products, snapping photos, or entering search queries. With real-time translation, users can instantly translate product labels using their phone camera, making international items easier to understand. The app also features a community-driven platform where users can share products, recipes, and photos with friends, fostering collaboration. FlavorIT's bookmarking feature allows users to save their favorite products for easy access later. By combining advanced technology and social features, FlavorIT creates a more personalized and accessible shopping experience.
