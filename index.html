Projects

Truvoye

The Truvoye web application is a logistics management platform that helps the logistic companies that want to simplify the delivery initiation process by minimizing waiting times for delivery initiation, allowing them to supervise operations quickly, onboard drivers and track orders in real time. Our goal is to produce the best estimates for your delivery needs and provide a definitive user experience that is optimized for the logistics needs.
